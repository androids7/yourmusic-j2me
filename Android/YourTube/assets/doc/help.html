YouTube Downloader 1.0.0

Developer: Oleg Derevenetz <oleg.derevenetz@gmail.com>

YouTube Downloader is an application for Android devices that lets you to download your favorite YouTube videos on your phone to watch them at any time without Internet connection.

Please note that download of some video categories (restricted video, video from official YouTube channels, such as VEVO, and so on) may not be supported due to YouTube Terms of Service.

If you have any questions regarding this application, you can contact application developer on this website: http://yourtube.sourceforge.net/.
